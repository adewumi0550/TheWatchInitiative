    About Us

    Our Vision

    We see a hunger-free and safer world for children, where every child is empowered to succeed.

    Our Mission

    Every day, we set out to create a safer and enabling environment for the overall well-being and development of unprivileged children around the world. Through our various initiatives, we strive to provide underserved children with sustained care and support to help guide them into becoming responsible adults and positive contributors to global peace and socio-economic development.

    What About The Children (WATCH) Initiative is a non-profit conceived in December, 2020 by a group of determined individuals who recognized the need to provide the needed care and support to underserved children around the world, by taking definite actions to alleviate the sufferings of indigent children in line with SDG. Our various initiatives and platforms provide us with solutions to seven challenges indigent children faces every day:

    Our Initiatives

    What About The Children (WATCH) Initiative

    FOOD

    This initiative is geared towards achieving zero-hunger for children around the world. Through our various food drive programs, we provide food and nutrition for underserved children every day.

    WATER

    It is said that about 71 percent of the earth surface is covered with water. But the sad reality is that most children especially those in developing countries lack access to clean water. Through WATCH Water Initiative, we quench the thirst of indigent children by providing them potable water.

    SHELTER

    There are millions of children who do not have a place called home. This initiative affords us the opportunity to place homeless kids around the world into good homes for their proper upbringing.

    FAMILY

    A common denominator amongst all children is the family. We understand the vital role a family could play in the life-development of a child. Through the WATCH Family Initiative, we empower less privileged families of children to become financially self-reliant, thereby breaking the shackles of child poverty.

    RIGHTS AND SAFETY

    The rights of children are prone to abuse if left unchecked. Through our Rights and Safety Initiative, we set out to safeguard the rights and privileges of children all around the world and to protect them from different forms of abuse and violence, based on the framework of the United Nations Convention on the Rights of Children.

    HEALTHCARE

    WATCH Healthcare Initiative is designed to provide underserved children around the world with free medical care. We believe that a healthy child is a happy child and therefore a catalyst for socio-economic transformation of societies. Through this initiative, we provide access to basic healthcare to less privileged children in communities, relieving them from the burden of lack of medical care for children in those communities.

    EDUCATION

    Education as it is often recognized around the world is the foundation upon which sound societies are built. Every child has the right to basic education. However, there are millions of primary school age children who are not enrolled into schools due to poverty and hostile environment. Through WATCH Education Initiative, we provide early childhood and basic education to unprivileged children through our sponsorship programs, provision of essential learning tools and conducive environment, and motivation of child-care givers around the world.
