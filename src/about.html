Our Vision

We see a hunger-free and safer world for children, where every child is empowered to succeed.

Our Mission

Every day, we set out to create a safer and enabling environment for the overall well-being and development of unprivileged children around the world. Through our various initiatives, we strive to provide underserved children with sustained care and support to help guide them into becoming responsible adults and positive contributors to global peace and socio-economic development.

What About The Children (WATCH) Initiative is a non-profit conceived in December, 2020 by a group of determined individuals who recognized the need to provide the needed care and support to underserved children around the world, by taking definite actions to alleviate the sufferings of indigent children in line with SDG. Our various initiatives and platforms provide us with solutions to seven challenges indigent children faces every day:

  • What About The Children FOOD
  • What About The Children WATER
  • What About The Children SHELTER
  • What About The Children EDUCATION
  • What About The Children HEALTHCARE
  • What About The Children FAMILY
  • What About The Children RIGHTS AND SAFETY

Our Core Values

What About The Children (WATCH) Initiative

TEAM WORK

We are a people-centered organization. We are about human connections and collaboration. We work together as one winning team, because we believe in Together Each Achieves More.

ACCOUNTABILITY, HONESTY AND TRANSPARENCY

We value the trust reposed on us by our donors and sponsors. We place premium on moral correctness. Therefore, we take responsibility to render good account of our stewardship in honest, transparent manner.

QUALITY SERVICE AND IMPACT

All our solutions are topnotch and tailored for lasting impact. We subscribe to excellence in service delivery.

SAFETY

We cherish the safety of our team and everyone working with us. This informs the precautionary measures that we put in place in executing our different plans and programs.
